Mindfulness-based Cognitive Therapy Workshop

This two day training workshop is for mindfulness teachers working in clinical or mental health contexts where MBCT is indicated as the mindfulness-based intervention of choice. 

We are offering this workshop to those who have already completed the Level 1 training or equivalent, and who wish to explore in more depth, the ways in which MBCT is similar yet different from MBSR.

 Some specific explorations will include: 

• Elaborating in more detail on the theoretical rationale for the use of MBCT in anxiety, depression and other mental health conditions

• Assessing suitability for this approach in mental health settings

• Pracitising different elements of the MBCT program which are specific to depression and anxiety

• Understanding how the term ‘therapy’ is applied within Mindfulness-based Cognitive Therapy, and how the inquiry may therefore differ 

• Learning from new findings and developments in the recent literature

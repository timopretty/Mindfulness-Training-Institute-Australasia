Helpful reading for the 1st assignment of the Study Module:

Books

  • Kabat-Zinn, J, Full Catastrophe Living, Using the Wisdom of Your Body and Mind to Face Stress, Pain and Illness. Delacorte Press, New York, NY, 1990.

  • Segal, Z. V., Williams, J. M. G., Teasdale, J. D. Mindfulness-Based Cognitive Therapy for Depression. 2nd Edition. New York: Guilford Press, 2013 
    • Chapters 4-18
  • McCown, Reibel and Micozzi. Teaching Mindfulness: a practical guide for clinicians and educators. Springer, 2010, 
    • Chapters 6 and 7

  • The introduction to Bhante Gunaratna’s free online book "The Four Foundations of Mindfulness in Plain English" (about 5 pages), is a useful overview of the four foundations for the purposes if the first assignment. If you scroll through the table of contents of the book and his acknowledgements, you will be able to read the introduction in this Google book format:  Click here to go to the online free version of the book.

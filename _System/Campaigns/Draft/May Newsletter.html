MTIA Newsletter May, 2014

Dear fellow travellers,

 

Welcome to our inaugural newsletter for the MTIA.  We would love your feedback about what you are interested in hearing about in the newsletter.  We would like it to be a forum for capturing current directions, ideas and developments in the world of mindfulness based interventions and especially training and pedagogy issues.

I have just returned from the Center for Mindfulness Conference in Boston where there were some wonderful and inspiring offerings, and some very good learnings for me, which I hope may be relevant to you too as teachers involved in bringing this work into different contexts.

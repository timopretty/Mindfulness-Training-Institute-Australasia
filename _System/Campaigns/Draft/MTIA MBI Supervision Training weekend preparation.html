MBI TAC exploration: Videos of your mindfulness teaching

Ideally we would use videos of external teachers in order to discuss various teaching points but have been unable to source these so we have a request.

We ask that those of you who are willing, to bring along a couple of excerpts from your recent recording of a class – perhaps one which demonstrates something that went well, and maybe one which you feel did not go so well.    We ask that it be of an inquiry, didactic or group process moment, rather than simply leading practices.   There is no pressure to present your own work if this doesn’t feel right for you.  If you are happy to do this, please bring your recording on a USB.

DAILY SCHEDULE

Our schedule is quite intensive, since we will be beginning our day before breakfast, and ending the day after dinner in the evening. Typically, we may begin morning practice at 7am and end with evening practice from 8.30 - 9pm. We will do our best to be responsive to the need for rest and self-care, but please note that this is truly an “Intensive” program, and we expect you to attend all sessions.

We will use didactic presentations, discussions, experiential exercises, feedback processes, contemplation and meditation. We will be working in the large group (about 34 people) sometimes, but mainly in smaller home groups and in dyads.

As in any retreat, the experience allows us to meet aspects of ourselves that may be new and unfamiliar, and yet times, all too familiar. We will guide and support you through this time, as will the warmth and safety of the smaller groups and the practice itself.

  • Umbrella/rain jacket
  • Insect repellent

  • Hot water bottles or wheat bags
  • Sitting Equipment: if you can, please bring your own sitting equipment (meditation cushion, stool, blanket/shawls, etc). If you are unable to do this, there will be some chairs, stools, cushions and mats available for your use
  • Yoga mat: do bring a yoga mat if you can; otherwise using the thick carpet will be fine

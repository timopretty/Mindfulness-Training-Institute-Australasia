Mindful movement workshop

Awareness of the body is central in teaching mindfulness meditation and in-depth experience in a body-based awareness practice is an important basis for teaching MBSR and MBCT.

This weekend is designed to deepen your understanding of the body and the teaching of body awareness through movement in your mindfulness classes.  An overview of basic safety practices in leading movement will be given drawing on an understanding of how the body moves  optimally and safely.

We will include:

  • exploration of postures: sitting, lying and standing
  • including and assisting people with difficulties
  • modifications for individual needs
  • facilitating safe exploration
  • leading practice with feedback
  • stewardship of the group
